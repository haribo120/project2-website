Who is Dr. Timnit Gebru?

Dr. Timnit Gebru is a computer scientist renowned for her groundbreaking work in AI ethics, algorithmic bias, and diversity in tech. As a co-founder of Black in AI, she has been a strong advocate for inclusion and equity in artificial intelligence research and industry.

She previously worked as a researcher at Google AI and co-authored a pivotal paper on bias in large-scale language models, highlighting the dangers of unregulated AI development. Following her controversial departure from Google, she founded the Distributed AI Research Institute (DAIR) to promote independent research in AI ethics.

Who is Megan J. Smith?

Megan J. Smith is an American engineer and technologist who served as the third Chief Technology Officer of the United States under President Barack Obama. She was previously a vice president at Google, where she co-created WomenTechmakers, an initiative to support women in technology. Smith has used her platform to advocate for diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.

🎥 Megan Smith on Technology, Government and Diversity

Megan's Allyship

During her tenure as CTO, Megan worked to promote inclusive policies and initiatives aimed at increasing representation of women, minorities, and other underrepresented groups in STEM fields. She emphasized the importance of diverse perspectives in driving innovation and pushed for equitable access to technology and education. Smith also co-founded the Malala Fund, which supports girls' education globally, and has been involved in various organizations and initiatives that empower marginalized communities in tech.
